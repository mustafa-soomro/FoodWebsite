Grilled Cheese


Grilled Cheese is a quick and easy recipe that could serve for any meal of the day. For a quick and easy meal, a grilled cheese is the best way to go!

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of white bread
  • 3 table spoons of butter
  • 2 slices of Cheddar cheese


Methods (2 Servings):

  1. Preheat pan over medium heat. Butter one side of a slice of bread and place the butter side down onto the pan and place a slice of cheese.
  2. Butter one side of a second slice of bread and place the butter side up on top of the sandwich.
  3. Grill until it turns a light brown. Then flip it over, and continue to grill the sandwhich until the cheese is melted.
  4. Repeat the steps with the remaining ingredients to make a second serving.
